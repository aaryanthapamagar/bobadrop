Boba Drop

Boba Drop is a sweet little treat that combines the fun of popping boba with the freshness of a chilled drink. Each drop is filled with fruity or creamy goodness that bursts in your mouth, making every sip an adventure.

Whether you add them to milk tea, lemonade, smoothies, or even desserts, Boba Drops bring a playful twist and a splash of flavor to your day. They’re colorful, chewy (or sometimes juicy), and perfect for sharing—or keeping all to yourself.
